Selroti

Description

Sel roti is a traditional Nepali fried bread or rice doughnut. It's made from a batter of ground rice, sugar, and sometimes flavorings like cardamom or banana. The batter is shaped into a ring and deep-fried until golden brown, resulting in a crispy exterior and a soft, chewy interior. It's often enjoyed as a snack or during festivals and special occasions in Nepal.

Ingredients

  • Rice flour
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Oil or ghee
  • Flavorings (optional)
  • Baking powder (optional)

Recipe

  1. Prepare the batter: Mix rice flour, sugar, mashed bananas, yogurt, and cardamom powder to form a thick batter. Let it ferment for a few hours.
  2. Fry the Selroti: Heat oil in a deep pan. Pour batter in a circular motion to form ring shapes. Fry until golden brown and crispy on both sides.
  3. Serve and enjoy: Drain excess oil and serve Selroti warm. Enjoy its delicious flavor and crispy texture!
